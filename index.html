About

I'm the Chief Technology Officer and one of the co-founders of Supercede. I primarily work with Haskell/Yesod and Nix, and I have been featured on The Haskell Interlude podcast.

For the past several years I have been slowly travelling the world while working, living in different places for a few months at a time. In January 2022 I decided to live full-time in Ukraine. Good timing!

I'm interested in building calm and sustainable companies with minimal process, a mostly flat hierarchy, and a lack of bureaucracy. This blog is loosely themed around all of the above.

I occasionally write my thoughts on this website. If you like, you can read my writing.
